## üí° **Naming Strategy Tips**

### **1. Keep It Simple**
- Short names (1-2 words) are easier to remember
- Avoid hyphens, numbers, or special characters
- Easy to spell and pronounce

### **2. Make It Relevant**
- Should relate to field service, operations, or work management
- Avoid generic names that don't convey purpose
- Consider your target audience

### **3. Check Availability**
- Domain (.com preferred, .app as backup)
- Social media handles (@name on Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
- Trademark (USPTO search)
- App store names

### **4. Consider SEO**
- Names with keywords can help SEO
- But don't sacrifice brandability for SEO
- Balance is key

### **5. Think Long-Term**
- Will it still make sense in 5 years?
- Can it grow with your business?
- Does it limit you to one niche?
